Portion Control on nutrition plan

  • VISUALIZE PORTIONS: Use cues like a deck of cards for meat and a tennis ball for fruit.
  • PLATE METHOD: Divide meals with half veggies, a quarter lean protein, and a quarter carbs for balance.
  • LISTEN TO HUNGER: Pay attention to fullness cues to avoid overeating.

Hydration on nutrition plan

  • WATER: Aim for 8-10 glasses daily to stay hydrated and adjust based on activity level.
  • HYDRATING FOODS: Include fruits, vegetables, and soups in meals for added hydration.
  • LIMIT SUGARY DRINKS: Minimize intake of sugary beverages to avoid excess calories and prioritize nutrient-rich options.

Balanced Macronutrient Intake on nutrition plan

  • PROTEIN: Include lean sources like chicken, fish, tofu, and legumes for muscle repair and satiety (20-30% of calories).
  • CARBOHYDRATES: Opt for complex options like whole grains, fruits, and veggies for sustained energy (45-65% of calories).
  • FATS: Incorporate healthy fats from nuts, seeds, and avocados for hormone balance and brain health (20-35% of calories).
